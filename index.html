Background

Imbuto Hub began as a part of the revolutionary movement to solve operational inefficiency in dairy cooperatives, a great source of farmer livelihood.

Mission

To empower dairy farmers and cooperatives with easy-to-use digital tools that enhance transparency, efficiency, and profitability across the entire dairy value chain.

Vision

A connected and thriving dairy ecosystem where every farmer, regardless of location or scale, has access to the resources, data, and support needed to grow sustainably and prosper.
